 Project Description

Cyber Central is a part of mixed-use development – Bhutani Cyberthum spread across an area of 27 acres. The project offers a wide range of ticket sizes commencing from 60 Sq.Ft. to 400 Sq.Ft. located on 8th and 34th floor of the iconic twin tower. Cyber Central will be equipped with state-of-the art sky mall, which is one of India’s largest musical fountains. Strategically located in the heart of Noida’s IT corridor, this project offers seamless connectivity to malls, schools and residences.
