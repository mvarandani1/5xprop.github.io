Noida is one of the biggest economic hubs in the NCR and is also a major housing destination. Noida has witnessed huge demand in the affordable housing category. This demand is majorly coming from professionals migrating to the city from Delhi and other cities in search of better employment opportunities. These professionals mostly look for reasonably priced, manageable and well connected projects near their places of work. These projects also cater to a huge pool of junior and mid level executives who also keep affordability at the forefront of their buying decisions. Several developers are now tapping into the potential of the affordable housing market in Noida which is home to a large working population.

You can find the best affordable apartment Noida in several locations including the Yamuna Expressway, Greater Noida West, Main Greater Noida, Noida East, Noida extension Tech Zone IV, Noida Extension Knowledge Park V and others. There are several projects being built by leading developers in these areas and finding affordable housing solutions will never be a problem with SquareYards at your service. The latent potential in the affordable housing market will be unleashed even further in the coming years with the development authorities and leading developers planning for several affordable housing clusters to be spread throughout the city.

Noida is home to several leading multinational companies, corporates and other startups. Noida has evolved into a major economic, commercial and business hub and this has led to rapid development of physical and civic infrastructure throughout the city. Major roads like the Dwarka Expressway, DND Flyway and the Noida-Greater Noida Expressway facilitate easy connectivity for residents along with the Delhi metro railway extension and other public transport facilities.
